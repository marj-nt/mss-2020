Who is Makenna?

Instructor Makenna Mason has been passionately swimming since the age of 6 and has taught and coached for 4+ years. She is certified in CPR, first aid, and AED.
Makenna believes that swimming is an important survival skill that can grow into a fun and competitive hobby! In the fall, she plans to attend Sacramento State as a graduate student in their Applied Behavior Analysis program. However, will continue to offer swimming lessons to spread the love of water to the community.
